Dear {{employee_name}},

This document serves as an acknowledgement that you have received, read, and understood the Company Code of Conduct of {{company_name}}. By signing this acknowledgement, you confirm your commitment to abide by the policies and standards outlined therein.

The Code of Conduct covers the following key areas:

1. Professional Behavior: All employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional conduct, treat colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with respect and dignity, and foster a collaborative and inclusive work environment.

2. Dress Code: Employees must adhere to the company's dress code policy, maintaining a neat, clean, and professional appearance appropriate to the workplace and their role.

3. Attendance and Punctuality: Employees are required to maintain regular attendance and adhere to prescribed working hours. Any absence or tardiness must be reported to the reporting manager in accordance with company procedures.

4. Use of Company Resources: Company resources, including but not limited to equipment, technology, internet, email systems, and office supplies, are to be used for legitimate business purposes. Personal use must be minimal and must not interfere with work responsibilities or violate any company policies.

5. Conflict of Interest: Employees must avoid any situation that creates or appears to create a conflict between their personal interests and the interests of the company. Any potential conflicts of interest must be disclosed to the management immediately.

6. Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption: The company maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward bribery and corruption in any form. Employees shall not offer, give, solicit, or accept any bribe, kickback, or improper payment, whether directly or through a third party.

7. Data Protection and Confidentiality: Employees are responsible for safeguarding confidential company information, personal data, and proprietary materials. Unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or distribution of confidential information is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action and legal consequences.

I understand that violation of any provision of the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
