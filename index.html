HR Documents – Company Checklist

Comprehensive list of documents a company should issue from an HR perspective

Sr. Category Document Purpose / Description
A. Pre-Employment & Hiring
1Pre-EmploymentOffer LetterOutlines role, CTC, joining date, and conditions of the offer
2Pre-EmploymentNon-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)Protects company's confidential information
3Pre-EmploymentNon-Compete AgreementRestricts employee from joining competitors for a specified period
4Pre-EmploymentBackground Verification ConsentAuthorization to verify educational, employment & criminal records
5Pre-EmploymentMedical Fitness CertificateConfirms employee is medically fit to join
B. Onboarding & Joining
6OnboardingAppointment LetterFormal employment contract with terms, salary, designation & policies
7OnboardingEmployee Handbook / HR Policy ManualCovers leave, attendance, code of conduct, grievance, anti-harassment policies
8OnboardingEmployee ID CardOfficial identification within the organization
9OnboardingPF Nomination FormNomination of beneficiary for Provident Fund
10OnboardingESI Nomination FormNomination for Employee State Insurance (if applicable)
11OnboardingGratuity Nomination FormNomination of beneficiary for gratuity payments
12OnboardingBank Account Opening LetterLetter to bank for salary account opening
13OnboardingInsurance Enrollment FormEnrollment in group health/life insurance
C. During Employment – Regular
14CompensationMonthly Salary Slip / Pay StubBreakdown of earnings, deductions, and net pay
15CompensationForm 16 / Tax CertificateAnnual TDS certificate for income tax filing
16CompensationIncrement / Revision LetterCommunicates salary hike and revised compensation
17Career GrowthPromotion LetterConfirms change in designation, role, and revised CTC
18Career GrowthTransfer LetterCommunicates change of department, branch, or location
19Career GrowthDeputation LetterAssigns employee to work at another location/entity temporarily
20PerformanceAppraisal / Performance Review LetterDocuments performance rating and feedback
21PerformanceConfirmation LetterConfirms regularization of employment after probation
22PerformanceProbation Extension LetterExtends probation period with reasons and new review date
D. Leave & Attendance
23LeaveLeave Sanction / Approval LetterFormal approval for leave applied by the employee
24LeaveMaternity / Paternity Leave LetterApproval of statutory maternity or paternity leave
25LeaveLeave Encashment LetterConfirms encashment of unused leave balance
E. Disciplinary & Compliance
26DisciplinaryWarning LetterFormal notice for misconduct, poor performance, or policy violation
27DisciplinaryShow Cause NoticeAsks employee to explain a specific act of misconduct
28DisciplinarySuspension LetterTemporarily suspends employee pending inquiry
29DisciplinaryTermination LetterFormal termination of employment with reasons and settlement details
30CompliancePOSH Policy DocumentPrevention of Sexual Harassment policy & Internal Committee details
31ComplianceCode of Conduct AcknowledgementEmployee's signed acknowledgement of company policies
F. Separation & Exit
32SeparationResignation Acceptance LetterFormal acceptance of employee's resignation with last working date
33SeparationRelieving LetterConfirms employee has been formally relieved from duties
34SeparationExperience CertificateCertifies designation, tenure, and conduct during employment
35SeparationFull & Final Settlement StatementDetailed statement of dues payable/recoverable at exit
36SeparationNo Dues CertificateConfirms employee has cleared all company dues and assets
37SeparationExit Interview FormCaptures feedback and reasons for leaving
G. Miscellaneous / On-Demand
38CertificatesEmployment Verification LetterConfirms employment for third parties (banks, visa, landlords)
39CertificatesSalary CertificateStates salary details for loan, credit card, or visa applications
40CertificatesBonafide CertificateConfirms person is a bonafide employee of the company
41CertificatesRecommendation / Reference LetterProvides a professional reference for the employee
42TravelAuthorization / Travel LetterAuthorizes business travel and related reimbursements
43TravelVisa Support LetterSupports employee's visa application for business travel
44InternalInternal Circular / Policy UpdateCommunicates changes in policies, holidays, or organizational updates
